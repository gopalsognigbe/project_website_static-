Ingredients

1 ½ cups (190g) all-purpose flour

1 ½ teaspoons baking powder

½ teaspoon salt½ teaspoon salt

½ cup (115g) unsalted butter, softened

1 cup (200g) granulated sugar

Instructions:

  1. Preheat Oven: Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C). Grease and flour an 8-inch round cake pan.
  2. Mix Dry Ingredients:Mix Dry Ingredients: In a medium bowl, whisk together the flour, baking powder, and salt. Set aside.
  3. Cream Butter and Sugar: In a large bowl, beat the softened butter and sugar together using a hand mixer or stand mixer until light and fluffy, about 2-3 minutes.
  4. Add Eggs and Vanilla: Add the eggs one at a time, beating well after each addition. Mix in the vanilla extract.
